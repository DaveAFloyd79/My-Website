Side projects!

About this page

I've worked on some side things that I've found interesting over the years. Some were ideas that made off the ground, others are still just random thoughts bubbling about in my brain that may never see the light of day. Or maybe they will.

Anyways, heres some things, click away.

Sometime around mid-2020, as we were all still coming to terms with lockdown and what it meant I stumbled across a Twitter post from Jess McQuade of WWF Cymru. Having just read the fantastic Doughnut Economics from Kate Raworth and seeing Jess was looking to get a group of people online to have a chat about the principles behind it and what it could mean in Wales I decided to pop along.

This led me signing up to the Wellbeing Economy Alliance Cymru and meeting some fantasticly inspiring individuals.

Late 2020 talk started to edge toward the upcoming Senedd elections. We pitched Some ideas to the room about how to get peole thinking about the notion of a wellbeing economy in Wales as they headed to the polling stations.

A few ideas seemed to have traction, indlcuing a Yes Cymru inspired 'newspaper' drop, a series of hustings and a voting app. The newspaper drop quickly fell by the wayside, as a volutneer organisation the costs were prohibitive. The ever positve and inspiring DawnLyle took forward a husting, with myself and Stephen Preistnalltaking a lead in developing an app.

Taking inspiration from the Future Generations of Wales Act, Duncan Fisher set about creating a set of wellbeing statements from organisaitons across Wales. The voting app was starting to take shape, with wireframe iterations using Google slides and developing into Whimsical as we started to make use of the Savvy platform, thanks in no small part to Alex of the ever dependanble WB40 podcast community. Eventually landing working prototype. The CMS for the app was all within Google Sheets making it a nice easy job to update content.
